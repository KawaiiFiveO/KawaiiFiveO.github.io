about

hi! i'm onehalf. welcome to my little website. here, you can find the projects that i've worked on, as well as more info about myself and how to contact me. click the buttons at the top to learn more.

i develop my software with the philosophy of "function over form".
i code in C#, Python, and occasionally C++ or Java.

i'm the founder of Llamaware, the modding group.

i don't just mod games, i play them too! here are some games i've played recently:
Limbus Company, Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune 5DX+/6, Re-Volt (RVGL),
Deep Rock Galactic, Split/Second, Ace Combat (PS2 trilogy)

you should read Umineko no Naku Koro ni, Remember11, and Ghost Trick: Phantom Detective.

if you made it this far, thanks for reading!
